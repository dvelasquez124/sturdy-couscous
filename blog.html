San Antonio, Texas

All about our trip to San Antonio, Sept 2023

The Alamo.

A UNESCO World Heritage Site located in downtown San Antonio. It was the first of the San Antonio missions founded to convert the local American Indians to Christianity.

This year, we went to San Antonio for my birthday and it was HOT. Here is a list of things we did while in San Antonio:

  • Riverwalk Boat Tour
  • Riverwalk Walking Tour
  • Self-guided tour of the San Antonio Missions by car
  • Visit the Alamo
  • Visit the Doseum
  • Go shopping at the Historic Market Square
  • Eat lots and lots of tacos and Mexican food!

If we each had to choose our favorite and least favorite activity it would probably look something like this:

Name Favorite Activity Least Favorite Activity
Jenssy Riverwalk Boat Tour Shopping
Sophie The Doseum The Alamo
Diana Shopping The Alamo

If you're short on time, I would skip the Alamo. Don't be fooled by my smile in the picture - I was miserable and ready to go back to the hotel to shower. As you can tell in this video, we were all smiles (especially, Sophie) indoors at the Doseum!

Orlando, Florida

Our first time in Orlando, June 2023

Walt Disney World.

An amazing resort in Orlando that has four theme parks and two water parks.

Earlier this year we took a trip to Orlando. Here is a list of things we did:

  • Magic Kingdom (duh!)
  • Disney Springs
  • SeaWorld
  • Relax at the amazing hotel
  • The Crayola Experience

If we each had to choose our favorite and least favorite activity it would probably look something like this:

Name Favorite Activity Least Favorite Activity
Jenssy SeaWorld Disney Springs
Sophie SeaWorld Magic Kingdom
Diana Magic Kingdom SeaWorld

If you're short on time, I would skip the Crayola Experience. It was fun but nothing to write home about. Sophie LOVED Sesame Street Land at SeaWorld. She had a lot of fun running through the sprinklers at Rubber Duckie Water Works. I would highly recommend staying at the Drury Plaza Hotel at Disney Springs.

Amsterdam, The Netherlands

Our first time visiting the Netherlands, November 2023

Amsterdam City Centre.

The historical centre of Amsterdam where many of the city's landmarks are located. There's plenty to do here: go shopping, take a canal tour or walking tour, or visit a museum!

This year, we went to Amsterdam for Sophie's birthday and, despite the rainy weather, we had a blast! Here is a list of things we did while in Amsterdam:

  • Private Photoshoot session in Amsterdam Centre
  • Walking and shopping in Amsterdam Centre
  • Anne Frank House Museum
  • Spa day at Van Der Valk Hotel
  • Canal Tour
  • Eat lots and lots of waffles!

If we each had to choose our favorite and least favorite activity it would probably look something like this:

Name Favorite Activity Least Favorite Activity
Jenssy Canal Tour Shopping. He hates shopping.
Sophie Eating waffles Anne Frank House Museum (it's not very toddler-friendly)
Diana Anne Frank House Nothing! I had a great time.
